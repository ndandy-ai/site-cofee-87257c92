Our Sourcing

At Obsidian Brew, every bean tells a story. We believe in transparency and ethical practices, which is why we partner directly with small-lot farmers from the world's most renowned coffee-growing regions. This direct-trade relationship ensures fair compensation for the growers and unparalleled quality in your cup. We focus on single-origin beans that express the unique terroir of their home, offering a tasting experience that is both honest and profound.
